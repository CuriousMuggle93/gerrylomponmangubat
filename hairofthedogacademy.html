Hair of the Dog Academy

  • Homepage
  • Portfolio
  • Project

I was responsible for maintaining and improving the Hair of the Dog Academy website, which was originally built with the Divi Theme on WordPress. My work included updating the site’s existing content and refreshing several of its key pages, while ensuring that all changes remained consistent with the design style established by the original designer.

Website maintenance and responsive layout improvements for enhanced user experience.

In addition to content updates, I focused on enhancing the site’s responsiveness and layout to provide a smoother experience across different devices. I also updated templates and blog posts to ensure the site remained fresh, functional, and aligned with the client’s evolving needs. These improvements strengthened both the usability and visual consistency of the site.

Nicole Begley, the owner of Hair of the Dog Academy, was very pleased with the results. The updates not only improved the overall user experience but also ensured the site continues to reflect the professionalism and quality of her brand.
