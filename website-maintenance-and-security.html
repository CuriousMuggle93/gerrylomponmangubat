What is Website Maintenance?

Hi, I’m Gerry—and I know that launching a website is just the beginning. To keep your site running smoothly, it needs regular maintenance and strong security.

Website maintenance ensures that your site stays updated, loads quickly, and functions properly, while security protects it from hackers, downtime, and data loss. Together, they give your business peace of mind and allow your website to serve your customers reliably every day. Without proper care, even the best-designed websites can face issues that affect performance and trust.

With my experience managing WordPress sites using platforms like WP Engine, WPMUDEV, and Cloudflare, I provide ongoing maintenance and security services that keep your website safe, reliable, and always ready to perform.


Processes

Regular Updates & Backups

+
-

Keeping your website up to date is essential for both performance and security. I manage WordPress core updates, theme and plugin updates, and set up automated backups to ensure your data is always protected.

If anything ever goes wrong, I can quickly restore your site to a previous version. This proactive approach prevents technical issues and keeps your website stable over the long term.

Content Updates & SEO Alignment

+
-

Your website’s content should evolve with your business. I help keep your site fresh by updating text, images, blog posts, product information, and landing pages.

These updates not only keep your audience engaged but also support better search engine performance. I make sure new content is SEO-aligned, so your site continues to rank well and attract the right visitors. With consistent content updates, your website stays relevant and competitive.

Website Security Monitoring

+
-

I take website security seriously. By using trusted platforms like Cloudflare and WPMUDEV, I monitor for vulnerabilities, block suspicious activity, and set up firewalls to keep your site protected.

I also implement SSL certificates and other protective measures to build trust with your visitors. With ongoing monitoring, I help minimize risks and safeguard your website against threats before they become problems.

Performance Optimization & Support

+
-

A slow website can turn visitors away before they even explore your content. That’s why I regularly optimize performance—compressing images, improving load times, and fine-tuning caching.

Alongside this, I provide troubleshooting and ongoing support to resolve issues like broken links, plugin conflicts, or layout problems after updates. These improvements not only create a smoother user experience but also give you peace of mind knowing your website is always in good hands.
