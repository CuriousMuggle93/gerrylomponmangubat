I collaborated closely with Dr. Joseph Cruise to refresh the LA Gynecomastia website by reimagining its UX/UI using WordPress and Elementor.

Modern UX/UI redesign via WordPress & Elementor to align with the client’s vision and elevate engagement.

My focus was on delivering a modern, clean design that closely aligned with Dr. Cruise’s vision—implementing intuitive navigation, updated layouts, and refined aesthetics. Throughout the process, I ensured the site remained fully responsive, guaranteeing a seamless experience whether visitors are browsing on desktop, tablet, or mobile.

The result is a website that feels fresh, approachable, and highly functional—exactly what the practice needed to elevate its online presence. Since launch, we've seen meaningful improvements in user experience and engagement, along with an encouraging boost in site traffic. The client has been very pleased with both the visual enhancements and the measurable performance gains.
