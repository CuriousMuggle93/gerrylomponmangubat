360Clean

  • Homepage
  • Portfolio
  • Project

I was tasked with maintaining and improving the 360Clean website, which was built on WordPress using the Divi Theme. My responsibilities included updating a large volume of existing content—particularly the location and services pages, totaling around 120 pages—as well as refreshing several of the site’s main pages. All updates were carefully executed to remain consistent with the design framework originally created by the site’s designer.

Website maintenance and large-scale content updates with responsive improvements for better user experience.

In addition to content updates, I introduced layout improvements to enhance the site’s responsiveness across devices. I also updated templates and blog posts to ensure the site remained functional, visually cohesive, and easy for users to navigate. These refinements brought a more polished and reliable experience to visitors while maintaining brand consistency.

While working part-time with Underdog Digital, the client expressed strong satisfaction with these improvements. The updates not only improved the website’s usability but also streamlined how information was presented, resulting in a smoother and more engaging user experience overall.
