What is UX/UI Design?

Hi, I’m Gerry—and beyond web development, I also specialize in UI/UX design. UI (User Interface) and UX (User Experience) design go hand in hand to ensure that a website or digital product isn’t just visually appealing, but also intuitive, user-friendly, and built around the needs of your audience. A well-crafted UI makes your site attractive and easy to navigate, while great UX ensures that visitors enjoy their journey, find what they need quickly, and take the actions you want them to—like signing up, purchasing, or contacting you. For businesses, strong UI/UX design leads to better engagement, higher conversions, and happier customers. I use tools like Figma and Adobe XD to design layouts and prototypes that bring ideas to life before development even begins. My focus is to bridge creativity with functionality, making sure every design decision supports your brand and your goals.

Processes

User-Centered Design Approach

+
-

When I design, I always put the end user first. I start by understanding your audience, their needs, and the challenges they face when interacting with your website.

This allows me to design layouts and experiences that are intuitive, enjoyable, and effective. Every button, form, and interaction is thoughtfully placed to make navigation simple and stress-free. By focusing on your users, I ensure your site delivers a positive experience that keeps visitors coming back.

Wireframing & Prototyping

+
-

Before jumping into development, I create wireframes and interactive prototypes using tools like Figma and Adobe XD. This helps you visualize the flow of your website and see how users will move through it.

Prototyping also allows us to make changes early, ensuring the final product is aligned with your goals. By mapping out the structure and functionality ahead of time, I save you both time and cost during the development process—while also making sure we’re building a site that works.

UI Design for Visual Impact

+
-

Your website’s first impression matters, and that’s where UI design comes in. I design interfaces that are clean, modern, and aligned with your brand identity.

From typography and color palettes to icons and imagery, I ensure every visual element reflects professionalism and builds trust. The goal is to create a design that’s not only aesthetically pleasing but also guides visitors naturally toward the actions that matter most to your business.
