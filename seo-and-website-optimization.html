What is SEO and Website Optimization

Hi, I’m Gerry—and I help businesses boost their online visibility through SEO and website optimization.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving your website so it ranks higher in search engines, helping the right audience find you. Website optimization, on the other hand, ensures that your site loads fast, runs smoothly, and provides a great user experience across all devices. Together, these two services give your business a competitive edge—driving more traffic, improving engagement, and turning visitors into customers.

With my expertise in tools like Yoast SEO, RankMath, Ahrefs, and Google Analytics, I make sure your website isn’t just attractive, but also search-friendly and performance-driven.


Processes

On-Page SEO Optimization

+
-

I optimize your website’s content and structure to improve search rankings and make it easier for people to find you online. This includes keyword targeting, meta tag optimization, internal linking, and schema markup.

By making your site more relevant and search-engine-friendly, I help increase organic visibility. My goal is to align your website with what your audience is searching for—so you get found by the right people at the right time.

Technical SEO Improvements

+
-

Behind the scenes, your website’s technical health plays a huge role in its success. I work on optimizing site speed, mobile responsiveness, crawlability, and fixing errors that may hold back your rankings.

Using tools like Screaming Frog and Google Search Console, I identify and resolve technical issues before they hurt your performance. A solid technical foundation ensures that search engines can index your site properly and users enjoy a smooth browsing experience.

Content & Keyword Strategy

+
-

Great SEO starts with great content. I help develop a keyword strategy that targets the terms your customers are searching for, then align your site content to those keywords.

Whether it’s blog posts, product descriptions, or service pages, I ensure your content is both engaging for readers and optimized for search engines. This balance between readability and SEO helps improve rankings while keeping your audience interested.

Performance Monitoring & Continuous Optimization

+
-

SEO and optimization aren’t one-time tasks—they’re ongoing processes. I set up analytics and tracking tools like Google Analytics and Ahrefs to monitor your site’s performance.

By analyzing data such as traffic sources, bounce rates, and conversions, I refine strategies and make adjustments over time. This continuous improvement approach ensures your website keeps performing at its best and continues delivering results long after the initial setup.
